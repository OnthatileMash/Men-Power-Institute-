Process & Research

From evaluation to employment — powered by data, driven by purpose.

Our 4-Step Process

1

Assessment & Research

Each graduate undergoes a comprehensive evaluation covering academic qualifications, soft skills, and career goals. Using our data intelligence system, we match each learner with relevant industries based on SETA-aligned standards and national skills demand data.

2

Strategic Matching

We use our proprietary Graduate-Industry Alignment Model (GIAM) to identify ideal placements for each candidate. Partnerships with employers across sectors ensure alignment between training outcomes and real-world needs.

3

Skills Preparation

Graduates complete tailored workplace-readiness programs designed to close the “skills gap.” This includes technical modules, digital literacy, professional communication, and ethical practice training.

4

Placement & Advancement

Our graduates are placed into meaningful employment, with continuous tracking to ensure growth, retention, and performance excellence. Post-placement support includes mentorship and career progression pathways.

Research That Drives Impact

Our decisions are evidence-based, informed by real-time data and industry analytics.

📊

Graduate Employment Analytics

We analyze national and regional employment trends, identifying where graduates struggle most — and designing interventions that directly address those challenges.

Key Insight: 73% of unemployed graduates lack exposure to industry experience — our model closes this gap through practical immersion.

🎯

Skills Gap Research

Our Skills Observatory Program tracks employer feedback and SETA reports to identify which skills are in highest demand across South African industries.

Example Finding: Employers report a 43% gap in problem-solving and communication skills — areas targeted in our readiness curriculum.

🔍

Market Intelligence Network

Through partnerships with private sector data firms and national agencies, we maintain a live feed of job market insights that continuously inform our placement models.

Ongoing Monitoring: 24/7 tracking of emerging roles, technologies, and growth sectors in the economy.

💼

Career Tracking & Impact Studies

We follow up with graduates post-placement to measure long-term impact, job satisfaction, and advancement rates — feeding this data back into our continuous improvement loop.

Result: 95% of MPI graduates remain employed beyond 12 months, with measurable income growth.

Turning Research into Real Results

Every insight we gather helps another graduate take their first step into the mainstream economy.
